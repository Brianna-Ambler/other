About This Journey

This Journey takes you from the fundamentals of large language models all the way to building and deploying production-grade Generative AI applications using Oracle Cloud Infrastructure. You will work hands-on with OCI Generative AI Service, Oracle AI Vector Search, and Oracle Database 23ai to build Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipelines and autonomous AI agents.

Each workshop in this path builds on the previous one, so you will finish with a complete, deployable AI application and a solid understanding of the underlying architecture. Whether you are an experienced developer new to AI or a data scientist looking to expand into cloud-native GenAI development, this Journey has been designed to meet you where you are and take you further.

What You'll Learn

  • ✓LLM fundamentals and prompt engineering
  • ✓OCI Generative AI Service APIs
  • ✓Vector embeddings and semantic search
  • ✓Building RAG pipelines with Oracle DB
  • ✓AI Agents and tool-calling patterns
  • ✓Deploying GenAI apps on OCI
  • ✓Monitoring and evaluating AI outputs
  • ✓Cost optimization for AI workloads
